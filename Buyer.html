Why B4Traders

B4Traders empowers you with direct access to premium inventory , published by trusted brands and retailers. Each listing comes with a detailed manifest so you know exactly what you're bidding on. You set the price—bid what it’s worth to you!

Step 1: Register Here

Choose the Brand or Retailer store you want to buy from. Read the terms of purchase and with few simple steps register for the store’s access.

Register as a buyer

Step 2: Get Approved

We will inform you as soon as your registration is approved. Our team will help you with the approval process
